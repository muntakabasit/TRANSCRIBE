# Privacy Policy for DAWT-Transcribe

**Last updated:** January 20, 2026

## Overview

DAWT-Transcribe ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our transcription service.

## Data We Collect

When you use DAWT-Transcribe, we process:

1. **Audio Files**
   - Audio recordings you create within the app
   - Audio from URLs you provide for transcription

2. **Transcription Data**
   - Text transcriptions generated from your audio
   - Language detection metadata
   - Timestamp information for segments

3. **Technical Data**
   - Device type and iOS version (for error logging)
   - App version information

## How We Use Your Data

### Audio Processing
- Audio files are sent to our secure backend server for transcription
- Audio is processed using OpenAI Whisper speech recognition
- **Audio files are NOT permanently stored** - they are deleted immediately after transcription

### Transcription Storage
- Transcription results are stored **locally on your device only**
- We do not store transcriptions on our servers
- You have full control to delete transcriptions at any time

### URLs
- When you provide a URL for transcription:
  - Our server downloads the audio from the URL
  - The audio is transcribed and immediately deleted
  - We do not store or log the URLs you submit

## Data Retention

- **Audio files:** Deleted immediately after transcription (typically within seconds)
- **Transcriptions:** Stored only on your device until you delete them
- **Server logs:** Retained for 7 days for debugging purposes only

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. **Fly.io** - Backend hosting
   - Purpose: Hosts our transcription API
   - Data shared: Audio files (temporary, deleted after processing)
   - Privacy policy: https://fly.io/legal/privacy-policy/

2. **OpenAI Whisper** - Speech recognition
   - Purpose: Converts speech to text
   - Data shared: Audio content (processed locally on our server)
   - Note: We run Whisper on our own servers, not through OpenAI's API

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your transcriptions with anyone
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your data with analytics companies

The only data transmission is:
- Audio sent to our backend for transcription
- Transcription results sent back to your device

## Data Security

We protect your data through:
- **HTTPS encryption** for all data transmission
- **Automatic deletion** of audio files after processing
- **Local-only storage** of transcription results
- **No account required** - no user data collection

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all transcriptions stored on your device
- **Delete:** Remove any transcription at any time
- **Export:** Share transcriptions in text or markdown format
- **Opt-out:** Stop using the service at any time

## Children's Privacy

DAWT-Transcribe does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Changes to This Policy
